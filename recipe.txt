Omelette
Time: 5 minutes
Category: Breakfast
- Beat eggs in a bowl.
- Pour into a heated pan, cook until set, and fold.
- Serve hot with desired fillings.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Time: 10-12 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
- Sauté minced garlic in olive oil until golden.
- Toss cooked spaghetti with garlic oil, red pepper flakes, and parsley.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich
Time: 5 minutes
Category: Lunch
- Butter bread slices.
- Place cheese between slices and grill until golden brown.
- Serve hot with tomato soup.

Greek Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onions.
- Toss with olives, feta cheese, and olive oil.
- Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.

Caprese Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Slice tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.
- Arrange on a plate with fresh basil leaves.
- Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil.

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
- Spread jelly on another slice.
- Press slices together to form a sandwich.

Tuna Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Mix canned tuna with mayonnaise and diced celery.
- Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
- Serve on a bed of lettuce or in a sandwich.

Fruit Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Dessert
- Chop assorted fruits like apples, bananas, and berries.
- Toss together with honey and lemon juice.
- Garnish with mint leaves.

Guacamole
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Mash ripe avocados in a bowl.
- Mix in diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and lime juice.
- Season with salt and pepper.

Pancakes
Time: 2-3 minutes per side
Category: Breakfast
- Mix flour, milk, eggs, and sugar in a bowl.
- Heat a greased pan and pour batter.
- Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden brown.

Caesar Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing.
- Add croutons and shaved Parmesan cheese.
- Serve chilled.

Tomato Bruschetta
Time: No cooking time
Category: Dinner
- Toast slices of bread.
- Top with diced tomatoes mixed with garlic, basil, and olive oil.
- Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Nachos
Time: 10-15 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Arrange tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
- Sprinkle with shredded cheese and black beans.
- Bake until cheese melts, then top with salsa and sour cream.

Hummus
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Blend chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil.
- Season with salt and cumin.
- Serve with pita bread or vegetables.

Garlic Bread
Time: 5-7 minutes
Category: Lunch
- Slice a baguette and spread garlic butter on each slice.
- Toast in the oven until golden and crispy.
- Serve hot.

Chicken Caesar Wrap
Time: 10 minutes (if chicken is not cooked)
Category: Lunch
- Fill a tortilla with sliced grilled chicken, romaine lettuce, Caesar dressing, and Parmesan cheese.
- Wrap tightly and serve.

Bruschetta
Time: No cooking time
Category: Dinner
- Toast baguette slices.
- Top with a mixture of diced tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil.
- Drizzle with balsamic glaze.

Margherita Pizza
Time: 12-15 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Spread tomato sauce on a pizza crust.
- Top with sliced mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
- Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Rice and Beans
Time: 20-25 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Cook rice according to package instructions.
- Heat canned beans in a saucepan with spices like cumin and chili powder.
- Serve beans over rice.

Quesadillas
Time: 5-7 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Place cheese and desired fillings (like cooked chicken or vegetables) on a tortilla.
- Fold tortilla in half and cook in a skillet until cheese melts and tortilla is crispy.
- Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Caesar Pasta Salad
Time: 10-12 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Cook pasta until al dente and rinse with cold water.
- Toss pasta with Caesar dressing, diced chicken, cherry tomatoes, and shredded Parmesan cheese.
- Chill before serving.

Baked Potato
Time: 45-60 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Wash and scrub potatoes.
- Rub with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
- Bake in the oven until tender, then serve with desired toppings like butter, sour cream, and chives.

Tabbouleh
Time: No cooking time
Category: Dinner
- Soak bulgur wheat in boiling water until tender.
- Mix with chopped parsley, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and mint.
- Dress with lemon juice and olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.

Deviled Eggs
Time: 10-12 minutes
Category: Breakfast
- Hard boil eggs and peel.
- Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks.
- Mash yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, and seasoning, then spoon back into egg whites.

Greek Yogurt Parfait
Time: No cooking time
Category: Breakfast
- Layer Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries in a glass.
- Repeat layers, ending with berries on top.
- Drizzle with honey if desired.

Tacos
Time: 15-20 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Cook seasoned ground beef or grilled chicken.
- Fill taco shells with meat, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
- Serve with lime wedges.

Spinach Salad
Time: No cooking time
Category: Lunch
- Toss baby spinach with sliced mushrooms, boiled eggs, bacon bits, and red onion slices.
- Dress with balsamic vinaigrette.

Vegetable Stir-Fry
Time: 10-15 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Heat oil in a skillet or wok.
- Stir-fry mixed vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots until tender-crisp.
- Season with soy sauce and serve over rice or noodles.

Cheese Quesadilla
Time: 5-7 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Place shredded cheese on a tortilla.
- Top with another tortilla and cook in a skillet until cheese is melted and tortilla is golden brown.
- Cut into wedges and serve with salsa and sour cream.

Tomato Soup
Time: 25-30 minutes
Category: Dinner
- Sauté diced onions and garlic in butter until soft.
- Add canned tomatoes, vegetable broth, and seasonings.
- Simmer for 20 minutes, then blend until smooth. Serve hot with a dollop of cream if desired.
